The Buying Process

We've made the process straightforward:

  1. Browse: Explore our available properties on the main page.
  2. Register: Sign up for a free account to access your personal dashboard.
  3. Inspect: We strongly recommend scheduling a physical site inspection. It's free of charge.
  4. Pay: Make a secure payment via Paystack or a direct bank transfer to our official company account.
  5. Get Your Documents & Property Allocation: Once your payment is confirmed, we begin processing your legal documents, which will be ready within 21 working days. Property allocation will follow on or before 3 months from the date of payment.

Payments & Security

You can pay through two secure methods:

  • Online Payment: Via Paystack, using your debit card or other online methods.
  • Direct Bank Transfer: By depositing directly into the official Prince Kaka Global Resources bank account listed on your dashboard.

Important: Never make cash payments to an agent or any individual. All payments must go through our official channels.

After making a transfer, please use the "Upload Payment Receipt" feature in your dashboard. You will need to provide the property details, amount paid, payment date, and a screenshot or PDF of your receipt. Our team will then verify it and update your account.

Yes. For online payments, we use Paystack, a leading payment processor in Africa that is fully PCI-DSS compliant, meaning they adhere to the highest security standards. We never store your card details on our platform. Your personal data is protected and used only for processing your transactions and managing your account.

Once your full payment is confirmed, we begin the documentation process immediately. You will typically receive your complete set of documents, including your Sales Receipt, Deed of Conveyance, and Registered Survey Plan, within 30 business days.

After a payment is made (especially via bank transfer), our team needs to verify it with the bank. This verification process can take up to 24 hours. Once confirmed, your new property will appear in the "My Land Investments" section of your dashboard.
